1. Privacy Policy

Insight Finance is committed to respecting the privacy of its clients, employees and other visitors who use our platforms, as well as the protection and security of their data.

Insight Finance recognizes that your security and privacy are very important, so we take all possible measures, within the best practices and existing solutions, to protect it. In this sense, we recommend that all of our employees are aware of our practices.

This Privacy Policy is intended to help you understand what information we collect, why we collect it and how you can exercise your right under Insight Finance's processing of your personal data, i.e. how we ensure the security of your information and how you can access it and request changes, for example.

2. Data collected

We collect and process information provided by you by filling in the forms available on our platforms, so we understand that you declare that you agree with our privacy policy, especially when you register and accept the terms and conditions of use of our platforms.

We collect the following types of information:

  • Identification data: name, email
  • Financial data: transactions, categories
  • Technical data: IP, browser, device, tokens

sensitive personal data:

  • IP: we identify the IP address number when accessing our platforms, in order to increase security, since, in the event of fraud, we will provide it to the competent authorities
  • Device information: we collect information about the model, operating system, preferred language, unique identifiers and serial number of the device
  • Financial Transaction Information: we collect financial transaction information to enable and verify the subscription to our services, as well as to provide the best use of our platforms to the user.

3. Data processing

Your data is used for:

  • Provide platform functionalities
  • Improving our services
  • Send important alerts
  • Developing and improving our services and products

All personal data and information will be treated as confidential, and only used for the purposes described and authorized herein, mainly so that you can make full use of our services, always aiming to improve our customers' experience. In this way, we can customize our services and products to increasingly tailor them to your preferences, needs and interests.

4. Bases legais

We have adapted all our processes that collect and process data to the legal bases set out in article 7 of the LGPD (General Data Protection Act).

The legal bases we use are:

  • Consent by the holder
  • Compliance with a legal or regulatory obligation by the controller
  • Execution of contract or preliminary procedures
  • Legitimate interests of the controller or third parties

5. Protection of personal data

Insight Finance informs that it values and continuously improves its security, technical and administrative measures necessary to protect the stored data against unauthorized access, accidental or illicit situations of destruction, loss, alteration, communication or any form of inadequate or illicit treatment, according to the technology currently available.

In addition, Insight Finance respects and protects your data in accordance with the main security standards and other Brazilian legislation. We apply the principles of Privacy By Design (privacy in development or from conception), processing data only with a guarantee of a high level of security and implementing the best data protection practices.

6. Responsibility of the holder of personal data

Our platforms may request permission to access services and files on your device, so please remember that any content you send to one of our platforms is your responsibility.

To ensure your safety, we would like to point out some very important precautions:

  • Protect your access credentials (passwords, electronic signatures, security codes, etc.), they are personal and non-transferable
  • Insight Finance does not contact the client by any means to request access passwords, electronic signatures, tokens or any security code or response.

In the event of any suspicion, please contact Insight Finance immediately via one of our main official channels.

7. Policy control

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time, however, we will notify you of any changes. However, it is advisable to review our Privacy Policy from time to time for any changes.

Changes to our Privacy Policy are effective when posted on this page.
